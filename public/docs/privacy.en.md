## Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Citizens' Hub and its related website, browser extension, and services (collectively, "Citizens' Hub", "we", or "our") collect, use, store, and share information about you.

## Information We Collect

Depending on how you use Citizens' Hub, we may collect:

- Account information, such as your email address, display name, avatar, login method, email verification status, and linked third-party sign-in information.
- Order and transaction information, such as order status, payment timestamps, invoice and payment-related records, fulfillment-related records, and support history.
- Information you provide directly, such as profile details, homepage links, RSI binding details, support ticket content, blog comment content, and avatars or attachments you upload.
- Extension and hangar-related data, such as hangar snapshots, ships, CCUs, bundles, and sync preferences that you choose to connect or upload. If you enable hangar sync, we also store revision numbers, update timestamps, device identifiers, content summaries, and conflict-resolution metadata needed to keep your hangar in sync.
- Device and usage information, such as error logs, basic feature usage data, required anti-abuse verification data, and request information related to captcha verification.

Some data is stored only in your local browser context. If you use account features, orders, attachments, shared hangars, or hangar sync, the related data is stored on Citizens' Hub servers or infrastructure providers in order to support accounts, orders, support, sharing, cross-device sync, and operations.

## How We Use Your Information

We may use your information to:

- Create and maintain your account.
- Process orders, payment confirmation, fulfillment, support, and fraud or abuse review.
- Save your preferences, sync settings, and hangar snapshots.
- When you enable hangar sync, store your private hangar snapshot and sync metadata remotely to support cross-device sync, revision history, and conflict recovery.
- Provide RSI account binding, sharing, customer support, email notification, and token-based integration features.
- Monitor errors, troubleshoot issues, improve the product, and prevent abuse.
- Use first-party data for advertising audience matching and ad targeting when you have explicitly opted in.

## Advertising Audience Matching and Optional Consent

If you actively opt in through the signup form or settings page, we may share certain first-party data, such as your account email address and first-party purchase status, with advertising platforms including Google for the following purposes:

- Building or refreshing customer audience lists such as Customer Match.
- Audience targeting, where permitted by law and where you have consented.

These options are optional and are not required to create an account or place an order. You can change them later in your account settings. If you withdraw consent, we will stop using that consent for future audience syncs, but data already received by a third-party platform may remain subject to that platform's systems and retention rules.

## Data Sharing

We may share necessary data with the following categories of third parties:

- Payment and transaction service providers, such as Stripe.
- Email and notification service providers.
- Cloud infrastructure, object storage, error monitoring, and hosting providers.
- Advertising platforms, such as Google, when you have explicitly opted in to audience matching or ad targeting.

Except as described above, we do not sell your personal information for a third party's own independent business purposes.

## Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect your information, including access controls, authentication, logging, and infrastructure security measures. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

## Data Retention

We retain data for as long as needed to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and prevent abuse. Retention periods may vary by data type.

## Your Choices and Rights

You can:

- Update your profile and advertising audience consent settings in the settings page.
- Request another email verification message.
- Clear locally stored extension or cache data from your browser where applicable.
- Request access, correction, or deletion of certain personal data where required by applicable law.

If you want to make a request regarding your account data, contact us using the information below.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will be posted in the website or app and will become effective when posted. You should review it periodically.
