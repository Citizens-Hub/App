## üíé Our Commitment

</div>

<table>
<tr>
<td>

We as members, contributors, and developers of the **Citizens' Hub** commit to make participation in our community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We are committed to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

</tr>

## üåü Our Standards

</div>

<table>
<tr>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

### ‚úÖ **Positive Behaviors**

<br>

```diff
+ Being respectful of differing opinions, viewpoints, and experiences
+ Giving and gracefully accepting constructive feedback
+ Accepting responsibility and apologizing to those affected by our mistakes
+ Focusing on what is best for the overall community
+ Showing empathy towards other community members
+ Helping new users understand the tool and Star Citizen CCU mechanics
```

</td>
<td width="50%" valign="top">

### ‚ùå **Unacceptable Behaviors**

<br>

```diff
- The use of sexualized language or imagery
- Trolling, insulting or derogatory comments
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information without permission
- Sharing exploits that violate Star Citizen's Terms of Service
- Other conduct inappropriate in a professional setting
```

## ‚öñÔ∏è Enforcement Guidelines

*Community Impact Guidelines for determining consequences*

</div>

<br>

<details open>
<summary><h3>üì¢ 1. Correction</h3></summary>

<table>
<tr>
<td width="30%" bgcolor="#E3F2FD">

**Community Impact**

</td>
<td width="70%">

Use of inappropriate language or other behavior deemed unprofessional or unwelcome in the community.

</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td bgcolor="#E8F5E9">

**Consequence**

</td>
<td>

A private, written warning from community leaders, providing clarity around the nature of the violation and an explanation of why the behavior was inappropriate. A public apology may be requested.

</td>
</tr>
</table>

</details>

<details>
<summary><h3>‚ö†Ô∏è 2. Warning</h3></summary>

<table>
<tr>
<td width="30%" bgcolor="#FFF3E0">

**Community Impact**

</td>
<td width="70%">

A violation through a single incident or series of actions.

</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td bgcolor="#FFE0B2">

**Consequence**

</td>
<td>

A warning with consequences for continued behavior. No interaction with the people involved, including unsolicited interaction with those enforcing the Code of Conduct, for a specified period of time. This includes avoiding interactions in community spaces as well as external channels like social media. Violating these terms may lead to a temporary or permanent ban.

</td>
</tr>
</table>

</details>

<details>
<summary><h3>üö´ 3. Temporary Ban</h3></summary>

<table>
<tr>
<td width="30%" bgcolor="#FFEBEE">

**Community Impact**

</td>
<td width="70%">

A serious violation of community standards, including sustained inappropriate behavior.

</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td bgcolor="#FFCDD2">

**Consequence**

</td>
<td>

A temporary ban from any sort of interaction or public communication with the community for a specified period of time. No public or private interaction with the people involved, including unsolicited interaction with those enforcing the Code of Conduct, is allowed during this period. Violating these terms may lead to a permanent ban.

</td>
</tr>
</table>

</td>
</tr>
<tr>
<td bgcolor="#F8BBD0">

**Consequence**

</td>
